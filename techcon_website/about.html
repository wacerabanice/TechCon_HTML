Mission

TECHCON 2024, a unique celebration of innovation and excellence, took place from September 8-10, 2024, at the Renaissance Hotel in Austin, Texas. This year's conference brought together a diverse group of industry leaders, undergraduate and graduate students, and technical recruiters. It was a platform for exchanging ideas, fostering collaboration, and celebrating achievements in the semiconductor industry. The conference was distinguished by the presence of two exceptional speakers who delivered insightful presentations that left a lasting impact on the audience.

History

CareerConnections at TECHCON 2024 CareerConnections is an intense 2-hour recruiting event. Technical staff and recruiters from SRC member companies are on hand to provide information to our SRC Research Scholars about each company. During this event, resumes and contact information are exchanged, while many companies actively recruit for open and upcoming job and internship opportunities. If you are looking to find a job or fill a job requisition, this is the place to do it!

Notable Past Speakers:

Notable speakers for various 2024 "TechCon" events included Mark Kashman and Josh Leporati (TechCon 365), Anne Locker and Hend ElGhazaly (Radio TechCon), and Jianheng Luo and Sanaz Sadeghi (TECHCON 2024 by SRC).
